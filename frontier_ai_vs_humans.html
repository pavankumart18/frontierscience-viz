Where AI Stands vs Human Researchers

AI now beats expert humans on closed-form science exams, competitive math, competitive coding, and protein-structure prediction. It still can't do real research — open-ended problems with novel reasoning, niche depth, and physical experiments. The gap is closing exponentially, but it is still a gap.

The 5-second read.
AI's ability to do a task doubles every ~4 months (METR, 2026). It already dominates on speed, scale, and standardized exams.
Humans still own open-ended, multi-step, hypothesis-driven research — the work that actually moves a field.
